## 定义

本节假设函数的陪域为度量空间。
涉及到级数时，因为需要加法结构，因此要假设陪域为Banach空间。
设计到柯西列时，需要用到完备性，因此要假设陪域为完备度量空间。

- **（任意集合 $\to$ 度量空间）** Suppose $\{f_n\}$, $n = 1, 2, 3, \dots$, is a sequence of functions defined on a set $E$, and suppose that the sequence of numbers $\{f_n(x)\}$ converges for every $x \in E$. We can then define a function $f$ by $$f(x) = \lim_{n \to \infty} f_n(x) \quad (x \in E).$$ Under these circumstances we say that $\{f_n\}$ converges on $E$ and that $f$ is the _limit_, or the _limit function_, of $\{f_n\}$. Sometimes we shall use a more descriptive terminology and shall say that “$\{f_n\}$ converges to $f$ _pointwise_ on $E$” if above condition holds.
- **（任意集合 $\to$ Banach 空间）** If $\sum f_n(x)$ converges for every $x \in E$, and if we define
  $$f(x) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} f_n(x) \quad (x \in E),$$
  the function $f$ is called the _sum_ of the series $\sum f_n$.
- **（任意集合 $\to$ 度量空间）** We say that a sequence of functions $\{f_n\}, n = 1, 2, 3, \dots,$ converges _uniformly_ on $E$ to a function $f$ if for every $\varepsilon > 0$ there is an integer $N$ such that $n \ge N$ implies $$|f_n(x) - f(x)| \le \varepsilon$$ for all $x \in E$.

## 性质

1. **（任意集合 $\to$ 完备度量空间）** The sequence of functions $\{f_n\}$, defined on $E$, converges uniformly on $E$ if and only if for every $\varepsilon > 0$ there exists an integer $N$ such that $m \ge N$, $n \ge N$, $x \in E$ implies $$|f_n(x) - f_m(x)| \le \varepsilon.$$
2. **（任意集合 $\to$ 度量空间）** Suppose $$\lim_{n \to \infty} f_n(x) = f(x) \quad (x \in E).$$
   Put $$M_n = \sup_{x \in E} |f_n(x) - f(x)|.$$ Then $f_n \to f$ uniformly on $E$ if and only if $M_n \to 0$ as $n \to \infty$.
3. **（任意集合 $\to$ Banach 空间）** Suppose $\{f_n\}$ is a sequence of functions defined on $E$, and suppose $$|f_n(x)| \le M_n \quad (x \in E, n = 1, 2, 3, \dots).$$ Then $\sum f_n$ converges uniformly on $E$ if $\sum M_n$ converges.
4. **（度量空间 $\to$ 完备度量空间）** Suppose $f_n \to f$ uniformly on a set $E$ in a metric space. Let $x$ be a limit point of $E$, and suppose that
   $$\lim_{t \to x} f_n(t) = A_n \quad (n = 1, 2, 3, \dots).$$
   Then $\{A_n\}$ converges, and
   $$\lim_{t \to x} f(t) = \lim_{n \to \infty} A_n.$$
   In other words, the conclusion is that
   $$\lim_{t \to x} \lim_{n \to \infty} f_n(t) = \lim_{n \to \infty} \lim_{t \to x} f_n(t).$$
5. **（度量空间 $\to$ 完备度量空间）** If $\{f_n\}$ is a sequence of continuous functions on $E$, and if $f_n \to f$ uniformly on $E$, then $f$ is continuous on $E$.
