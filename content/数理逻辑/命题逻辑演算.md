## 命题逻辑演算

- Formal system(形式系统) $L$ of statement calculus(命题演算):
  - Alphabet(字母表)(是countable的): $\{~,\rightarrow,\leftrightarrow,(,)\}\cup\{p_i|i\in\mathbb{N}\}$
  - Well-formed formula(良形公式)(wf):
    - $p_i$ is a wf
    - if $A$ and $B$ are wf, then $\neg A$ and $(A\rightarrow B)$ are wf
    - The set of all wfs generated by the above rules is called the set of wfs.
  - Axioms(公理):
    - (L1) $(A\to(B\to A))$
    - (L2) $((A\to (B\to C))\to ((A\to B)\to (A\to C)))$
    - (L3) $((\neg A\to \neg B)\to (B\to A))$
  - Rules of reduction(归约规则):
    - MP(modus ponens) Rules of deduction: from $A$ and $(A\to B)$, $B$ is a direct consequence where $A$ and $B$ are wfs of $L$.
- proof(证明): A proof in $L$ is a sequence of wfs. $A_1,A_2,\cdots,A_n$ such that for each $i(1<i<n)$, either $A_i$ is an axiom of $L$ or $A_i$ follows from two previous members of the sequence, say $A_j$ and $A_k$ ($j<i, k<i$) as a direct consequence using the rule of deduction MP. Such a proof will be referred to as a proof of $A_n$ in $L$, and $A_n$ is said to be a **theorem** of $L$.
- deduction(归约): let $\Gamma$ be a set of wfs. of $L$ (which may or may not be axioms or theoremsof L). A sequence $A_1,A_2,\cdots,A_n$ of wfs. of L is a deduction from $\Gamma$ (记作$\Gamma\vdash_{L} A_n$) if for each $i(1<i<n)$, one of the following holds:
  - $A_i$ is an axiom of $L$
  - $A_i$ is a member of $\Gamma$
  - $A_i$ follows from two previous members of the sequence, say $A_j$ and $A_k$ ($j<i, k<i$) as a direct consequence using the rule of deduction MP.
- valuation(赋值): A valuation of $L$ is a function $v$ whose domain is the set of wfs. of $L$ and whose range is the set $\{T, F\}$ such that, for any wfs. $A, B$ of $L$,
  - $v(A) \not = v(\neg A)$
  - $v(A\leftrightarrow B) = F$ if and only if $v(A) =T$ and $v(B) = F$
- tautology(重言式): A wf $A$ is a tautology if $v(A) = T$ for every valuation $v$ of $L$.
- extension(扩张): An extension of $L$ is a formal system obtained by altering or enlarging the set of axioms so that all theorems of $L$ remain theorems (and new theorems are possibly introduced).
- consistent(一致): A extension of $L$ is consistent if there is not exist a wf $A$ of $L$ such that $A$ and $\neg A$ are both theorems of extension of $L$. 即对所有的 wf. $A$ 都有 $A$ 或 $\neg A$ 不是 theorem of extension of $L$。
- complete(完备): A extension of $L$ is complete if for every wf. $A$ of $L$, either $A$ or $\neg A$ is a theorem of extension of $L$.

#### 性质

1. $\vdash_{L} (A \to A)$,where $A$ is any wf of $L$
2. Deduction theorem: $\Gamma \cup \{A\} \vdash_{L} B$ $\Longleftrightarrow$ $\Gamma \vdash_{L} (A\to B)$
3. (Hypothetical syllogism): $\{(A\to B),(B\to C)\}\vdash_{L} (A\to C)$
4. $\vdash_{L} (\neg B\to(B\to A))$
5. $\vdash_{L} ((\neg A\to A)\to A)$
6. Every theorem of $L$ is a tautology.
7. L is consistent.
8. An extension $L^*$ of L is consistent if and only if there is a wf. which is not a theorem of $L^*$.
9. 设$L^*$是$L$的一致扩张，$A$是$L$的一个 wf.，且$A$不是$L^*$的定理，则向$L^*$中添加$\neg A$作为公理，所得的扩张$L^{**}$也是一致的。
10. Let $L^*$ be an extension of $L$. Then there is a consistent complete extension of $L^*$
11. If $L^*$ is a consistent extension of $L$ then there is a valuation in which each theorem of $L^*$ is true.
12. (The Adequacy Theorem for $L$): If $A$ is a wf. of $L$ and $A$ is a tautology, then $A$ is a theorem of $L$.
